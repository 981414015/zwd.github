Gaming

DOTA

DOTA2 is an esports game which played in matches between two teams of five players, with each team occupying and defending their own separate base on the map. The game is a sequel to Defense of the Ancients (DotA), a community-created mod for Warcraft III. During every game, each of the ten players independently controls a powerful character known as a "hero" that all have unique abilities and differing styles of play. During a match players collect experience points and items for their heroes to defeat the opposing team's heroes in player versus player combat.

I started playing Dota since the game was still on Warcraft III when I was 8 years old. Before transitioning to Dota 2 on Steam in 2014, I had been playing the game for around 9 years. According to the record on steam, I have more than 11000 hours on DOTA2 till today. Others

I also love playing other strategy/card games. One is called Hearthstone, which is a famous online card game made by Billiards. The game is a turn-based card game between two opponents, using constructed decks of 30 cards along with a selected hero with a unique power. I have reached the highest rank: "Legend", for 4 reasons using 4 different decks including Paladin, Priest, Rogue and Druid. My favorite hero is Rogue, and I like to build my deck around it because Rogue's cards offer the hardest but highest reward combos to chain together. I have also achieved 12 wins multiple times in the Arena game mode of Hearthstone.

Another one is called DOTA Auto Chess, which is a game mode separated from the main DOTA2 mode; it's a strategy game mode. I have reached the highest rank: "Queen", for 4 reasons. My favorite style for this game is playing Aqir.

Besides these two card/strategy games, I play another survival horror game called Dead by Daylight. I have reached the highest rank of DbD for multiple seasons too.
